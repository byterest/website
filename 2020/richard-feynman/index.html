Quotes From Richard Feynman

May 11 2020 quote

The quotes is from https://twitter.com/ProfFeynman

  1. It doesn’t matter how beautiful your theory is, it doesn’t matter how smart you are. If it doesn’t agree with experiment, it’s wrong. In that simple statement is the key to science.

  2. Stupidity is knowing the truth, seeing the truth but still believing the lies.

  3. Failure is an incredible learning experience. It teaches you humility. It teaches you to work harder. It is the first step to understanding.

  4. Your ability to convey it to another is the ultimate test of your knowledge. If you cannot explain something in simple terms, you don’t understand it yourself.

  5. Your degrees aren’t as valuable as your skills. Brain

  6. Study hard what interests you the most in the most undisciplined, irreverent and original manner possible.

  7. You are under no obligation to remain the same person you were a year ago, a month ago, or even a day ago. You are here to create yourself, continuously.

  8. Understand, don’t memorize.

  9. We never are definitely right, we can only be sure we are wrong.

  10. Your best teacher is your last mistake.

  11. When you are dead, you don’t know you are dead. It’s pain only for others. It’s the same thing when you are stupid.

  12. Make mistakes and learn! Being wrong isn’t a bad thing like they teach you in school. It is an opportunity to learn something.

  13. There is no shame in not knowing. It’s Okay to say “I don’t know.” The problem arises when stupidity and irrational thoughts take the place left by ignorance.

  14. Science doesn’t really care about your beliefs.
